## Extraction Details

- **Price:** From Shopify variant/price, BigCommerce product price, Fairphone product page (€).
- **Brand:** From Shopify `vendor`, BigCommerce product metadata, or inferred from product name.
- **Color:** From Shopify variant options when available; often not present in demo stores.
- **Warranty:** From `policy_scraper` (live fetch from store policy pages) or `REAL_POLICY_BY_SOURCE` for known merchants.
- **Shipping:** From `/policies/shipping-policy` (Shopify), `/shipping-info` or `/returns` (BigCommerce).
- **Returns:** From `/policies/refund-policy` (Shopify), `/returns` (BigCommerce), or Fairphone refund policy.
